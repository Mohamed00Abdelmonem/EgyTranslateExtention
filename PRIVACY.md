# Privacy Policy

EgyTranslat does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal user data.

The extension only processes selected English words and sends them to the Free Dictionary API for retrieving definitions and examples.

Translation results may be cached locally on the user's device using Chrome storage for faster performance.

EgyTranslat does not collect browsing history, personal information, passwords, financial information, or any sensitive user data.

All processing is limited to the extension’s single purpose: translating English words into Egyptian Arabic colloquial meanings.
